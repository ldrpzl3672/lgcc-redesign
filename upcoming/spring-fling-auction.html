Featured Items

Trips & Special Experiences

The featured travel packages are split between the Thursday kickoff auction, the Saturday auction, and the on-course hole-in-one opportunity.

Thursday Kickoff Auction

Waste Management Open 2027

A luxury suite experience overlooking the 18th hole at the Phoenix Open in Scottsdale.

  • Two wristbands for the Raise Away suite on the 18th hole
  • One guaranteed day: Thursday or Sunday, subject to availability
  • Open bar in the suite
  • Breakfast and lunch buffet included

This package is auctioned during the Thursday kickoff event. Airfare and hotel are not included.

Saturday Auction

Gems of Mexico

A winner’s choice 7-night getaway at The Mayan Palace in Riviera Maya, Nuevo Vallarta, or Puerto Peñasco.

  • 7-night stay in a Studio Master Suite
  • Accommodates up to 4 people
  • Designed for 2 adults and up to 2 children under 12
  • Booking assistance provided

This package is auctioned on Saturday. Resort fees and taxes may apply at redemption.

Hole-in-One Prize

Gourmet & Golf Sonoma Retreat

A Sonoma getaway featuring a luxury stay, golf for two, Michelin-rated dining, and wine tasting.

  • 2-night stay at Fairmont Sonoma Mission Inn & Spa
  • 1 round of golf for two with cart
  • Dinner for two at Santé
  • Wine tasting for two at Chateau St. Jean

This trip is the hole-in-one prize during tournament play. If no one wins it, it becomes a Saturday auction item.
